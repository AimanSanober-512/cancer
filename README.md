AI CANCER DETECTOR SYSTEM
Cancer is a collection of diseases in which abnormal cells grow rapidly and may spread to various parts of the body. It is among the major causes of death globally, primarily due to delayed diagnosis and insufficient timely treatment. Detecting cancer at an early stage is essential for improving survival rates and lowering the cost of treatment.
However, conventional diagnostic methods depend on skilled medical professionals, advanced equipment, and considerable time… which are not always accessible in all regions, particularly in rural and underserved areas. As a result, many patients are diagnosed at advanced stages, where treatment becomes more challenging and less effective.
Artificial Intelligence (AI) offers the potential to address these challenges... .by processing vast amounts of medical data, identifying patterns, and delivering faster and more precise predictions. AI can support healthcare professionals in detecting cancer at an early stage... minimizing human errors and enhancing clinical decision-making.
Therefore, there is a strong need to develop an AI-driven cancer detection system... that can deliver early, reliable, and accessible diagnosis... to enhance patient outcomes and strengthen healthcare systems.
